About Us

Welcome to The 1st Student Chapter of SPE at Ausc

The 1st student chapter of Society of Petroleum Engineers at Al-Azhar University (SPEAUSC) that was founded in June, 2005 by students at the Petroleum Engineering Department.

We are a non-profit organization that aims to enhance the students' skills, share knowledge among them, provide students with all information related to their career, and help them to meet their goals.

Our Vision

To be an effective and outstanding student chapter that supports the oil industry in Egypt and all over the world.

To become the best student chapter in the world.

2

Our Mission

Educate & Train

To educate and train the future petroleum engineers to gain more technical information, soft skills, research in the field, serve the industry through professional knowledge, and finally to have an outstanding student chapter Award.

Introduce New Ideas

To introduce new brainstormed ideas and inspirational events that can change the thoughts and dreams of our audience. Hence, this will help them to choose the right careers and the perfect lifestyles.

Create Student Mentality

To create a student mentality that serves the community to be ready to understand and face the challenges of our society.

Community of Thinkers

To collaborate with our partners and sponsors from other universities and organizations to create a community of thinkers who believe in the power of ideas and that these ideas can change people's lives, perspectives, attitudes, and ultimately change the world.

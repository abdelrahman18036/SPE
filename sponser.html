Main Sponser

Chemical & Fertilizers Export Council

It is an entity affiliated with the Export Development Authority, which includes all the parties concerned with export, to play its role in implementing the strategy of the Ministry of Trade and Industry to increase the rates of chemical exports and fertilizers by promoting exports through online promotion methods and promotional missions and organizing business meetings, as well as building and developing export capabilities by The way of training and technical support and its objectives the sustainable growth of Egyptian exports And its importance Encouraging a shift towards high-value-added exports Expanding the exporter base to include small and medium enterprises Access to markets with promising export opportunities Simplifying procedures and creating a business environment to serve exporters.

It is chaired by Eng.Khaled Abu Al-Makarem, as well as the President of Makarem Tex, a member of the Board of Trustees of the Egyptian Foundation for Small Enterprise Development, and one of the leading industrial & commercial figures in Egypt.
